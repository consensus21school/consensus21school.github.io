Consensus 21 Peninsula

The Consensus21.School Peninsula Pilot Program is the first in a global network of schools operating on the Consensus21 protocol. This pioneering initiative sets the foundation for a decentralised, community-driven approach to education, where governance, funding, and curriculum development align with the principles of transparency and collective decision-making. By leveraging the Consensus21 protocol, the network aims to create a scalable and autonomous model for future schools, empowering students and educators alike. The Peninsula Pilot serves as the blueprint for this vision, demonstrating how decentralised education can thrive in the real world.
