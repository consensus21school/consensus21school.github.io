CONSENSUS 21 

Consensus21 is an open-source educational protocol that decentralises learning, removing hierarchies, standardised tests, and tuition debt. It fosters self-directed, project-based learning with mentor support, empowering learners with critical thinking, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving skills.
