CONSENSUS21 

Consensus21 is a new open source educational protocol built on decentralisation, eliminating traditional hierarchies, standardised testing, and tuition debt. The protocol promotes self-directed, project-based learning, guided by mentors within a flexible, community-driven framework.

The protocol's modular structure fosters creativity, while its Bitcoin-powered financial system ensures long-term sustainability. Consensus21 focuses on developing critical thinking, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving skills, preparing learners for the future.
