Consensus 21.

School

Consensus21 is an open-source educational protocol that decentralises learning, removing hierarchies, standardised tests, and tuition debt. It fosters self-directed, project-based learning with mentor support, empowering learners with critical thinking, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving skills.

The Consensus21.School Mornington Peninsula Pilot Program is the first step in building a global network of schools powered by the Consensus21 protocol. This program is all about creating a community-driven, decentralised approach to education—where decisions about governance, funding, and curriculum are made transparently and collectively.
