Why We Use Nostr

Most platforms own your data, control what is seen, and decide what gets removed.

Nostr is different. It teaches you how to own your digital identity, how publishing really works, how to build without asking permission, and how to leave a permanent record of your learning.

This is digital literacy at the protocol level, not just "internet safety tips".
