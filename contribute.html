Contribute to C21

At Consensus21, we believe in total transparency and community involvement when it comes to managing funds. To ensure complete accountability, we utilise a multisig wallet with a 3 of 6 signature requirement, meaning each transaction is verified by at least three members of the team. Our goal is to raise 10 BTC, and all contributions and fund movements are publicly visible, providing full transparency to our supporters.
