Brains Trust

The "Brains Trust" at Consensus21 is a select group of visionary individuals assembled before the school officially launches, tasked with helping to shape its foundational principles and framework. This group will collaborate to refine the decentralised education protocol, define the structure of self-directed learning, and ensure the integration of a Bitcoin-powered financial system. Joining the Brains Trust offers an opportunity to be part of a pioneering project from the ground up, influencing the development of a school that challenges traditional education systems and prepares students for a rapidly changing world. People may want to apply to be part of this influential group to have a direct role in creating an innovative and sustainable educational model.
